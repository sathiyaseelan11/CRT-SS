Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  1. 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  2. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  3. 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  4. 1 cup warm milk (110Â°F)
  5. 1/3 cup unsalted butter, melted

For the filling:

  1. 2 large eggs
  2. 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  3. 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed

Instruction

  1. mix the milk with the yeast
  2. melt the better and add to the mixture
  3. add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. knead the dough for 10 minutes
  5. transfer the dough into large bowl and cover with a damp cloth
  6. leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours
